Who We Are

At FoodLoop, we connect restaurants, weddings, and events with NGOs and communities to donate surplus food instead of wasting it. Our government-funded platform makes food donation easy, and also donors receive a compensation as an incentive. Join us in reducing food waste and helping those in need, while the government supports your efforts.

With a user-friendly platform, we simplify the food donation process, making it efficient, impactful, and sustainable. Join us in our vision to build a hunger-free world.
