Privacy Policy

FoodLoop is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details like name, email, and contact information during registration.
  • Donation details and preferences for food donations.
  • System usage data to improve our platform.

How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate donations and distributions.
  • To communicate with users, admins, and delivery personnel.
  • To improve user experience and ensure platform security.

Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent. For more details, contact us via the Contact Us page.
